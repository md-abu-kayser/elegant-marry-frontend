FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our wedding services and planning process.

What's the difference between event design and event planning?

Event design and event planning work hand in hand to deliver a successful event. Event design focuses on the visual and aesthetic aspects, while event planning handles logistics, coordination, and execution. We incorporate both to create your perfect wedding experience.

How early do we need to start planning an event?

We recommend starting the planning process 9-12 months in advance for optimal venue and vendor availability. However, we can accommodate shorter timelines with our expedited planning services.

What is your refund and cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation policies with full refunds available up to 90 days before the event. Between 90-30 days, a 50% refund applies. Within 30 days, specific terms apply based on vendor agreements.

How do you keep budgets on track?

We provide detailed budget planning tools, regular financial updates, and work with preferred vendors to ensure competitive pricing. Our transparent pricing model helps you stay informed and in control throughout the process.
